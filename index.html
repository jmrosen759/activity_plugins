Exploring WordPress Customization

4/6/2026

I chose the Astra theme and the WP Fastest Cache plugin. I mainly picked Astra because it just looked clean and wasnt hard to figure out, which made it easier to mess around with the settings. I went with WP Fastest Cache because it helps with site speed, and that seemed like something every site should have. After setting everything up, the site looked better and felt more put together, and I got a better idea of how WordPress customization actually works
